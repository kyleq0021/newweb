Who is Claire?
Why is she passionate about Eclairs?

Claire is anyone who looks at an Eclair
and knows, truly knows, that they are looking
at the work of a true craftsman...
Someone who knows that this is just an Eclair's world
and they're only living in it.
Just a humble stomach to which the eclair might
one day have the decency to bless with its presence
We are all Claire.
